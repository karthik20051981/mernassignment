About Agentblazer Club

We’re a student‑led community dedicated to learning by building. Our mission is to empower members with practical skills, mentorship, and opportunities to showcase their work.

Mission

To foster a collaborative environment where students can explore, experiment, and excel in modern technologies and AI‑powered systems.

History

Founded in 2024, the Agentblazer Club has organized workshops, campus tools, and inter‑college collaborations, growing into a vibrant community of makers and mentors.

Values

  • Curiosity: Ask questions and dive deep.
  • Community: Learn together and uplift others.
  • Craft: Build with care and ship with pride.
